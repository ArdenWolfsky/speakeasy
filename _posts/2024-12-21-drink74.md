## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz Fresh Coffee - right from [Rocky's Roastery](https://rockysroastery.com/)!
- 1 oz Whip cream Vodka
- 1 oz Espresso Liqueur
- 2 oz Eggnog
- Shake with ice
- Serve with a white chocolate rim & crushed peppermint

## Ingredients
* 1.5 oz Jameson Whiskey
* 2 oz Baileys
* 1.5 oz Coffee Liquor (or Mint)
* 3 oz Half and half
* A few drops of green food coloring/Luster Dust
* Shake over ice
* Top with whip cream and sprinkles

## Grape Base
* 2 oz Grape Pucker
* 1 oz Simple Syrup
* Shake over Ice

## Pineapple Base
* 2 oz Pineapple Rum
* 4 oz Pineapple Juice
* 0.5 oz Lemon Juice
* 1 oz Pasteurized Egg Whites
* Shake Over Ice

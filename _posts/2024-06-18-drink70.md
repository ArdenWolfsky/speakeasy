## Ingredients
- 0.5 oz Lemon Juice 
- 1 oz Orange Liqueur 
- 2 oz Cran-Tropical Juice
- 2 oz Citron Vodka 
- 1/8 tsp Edible Luster Dust (Pink or White)
- Shake with ice
- Garnish with Rainbow Rope Candy 

## Non-Alcoholic
- 0.5 oz Lemon Juice 
- 1 oz Orange Simple Syrup 
- 2 oz Cran-Tropical Juice
- 2 oz Lemon Lime Soda 
- 1/8 tsp Edible Luster Dust (Pink or White)
- Shake with Ice
- Garnish with Rainbow Rope Candy

## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz Whipped Vodka
- 1.5 oz Coconut Rum
- 2 oz Half & Half
- 0.5 oz Cream of Coconut
- Splash of Pineapple Juice

## Make
1. Combine all ingredients and shake with ice.
2. Serve over ice.
3. Garnish with Marshmallow pieces cut diagonally and dipped in pink sprinkles.

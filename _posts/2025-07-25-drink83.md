## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz Vodka 
- 2 oz Limoncello
- 0.25 oz Simple syrup 
- 1 oz Lemon juice 
- Shake with ice
- Top with sparkling lemonade
- Garnish glass with fresh lemon zest and sugar rim

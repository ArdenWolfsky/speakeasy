## Rosemary Syrup
* 0.5 Cup water
* 0.5 Cup sugar
* 3 Rosemary sprigs
* Boil together and let cool

## Ingredients
* 3 oz Vodka
* 1 oz Rosemary Syrup
* 0.5 oz Lemon Juice
* Shake with ice Top with Ginger beer
* Garnish with Rosemary and Cranberries

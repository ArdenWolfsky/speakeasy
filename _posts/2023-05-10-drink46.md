## Ingredients
* 4 oz Pineapple juice
* 3 oz Gin
* 0.5 oz Lemon juice
* Shake with ice
* 2 oz Raspberry Lambic
* Fill glass with ice
* Pour mix over ice
* Garnish with fresh lime and mint
